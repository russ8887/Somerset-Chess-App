How Camp Events Work

Multi-Day Events:

Separate events will be created for each day of the camp (e.g., "Year 4 Camp - Day 1", "Day 2", etc.)

Year Level Targeting:

  • Select one or more year levels
  • All classes in those year levels will be affected
  • Students will be marked absent with "School Camp" reason
  • Events are processed automatically when coaches view dashboards
